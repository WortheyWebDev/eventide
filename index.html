A decorated war hero and leader of Nova Locus' military forces. Haunted by the loss of his family during the Great War, Elias is determined to protect the colony at all costs.

DR. LILA MERCER

A brilliant scienist specializing in nuclear energy and quantum physics. Dr. Mercer and her team of scientists are the first to step through the threshold.

A mysterious operative from the shadowy organization known as The Eclipse Initiative, tasked with investigating and containing supernatural threats.

GAMEPLAY

EXPLORE A WORLD ON THE BRINK

Every corner of Nova Locus is filled with lore and mystery. Pay attention to your surroundings, as the environment itself tells a story of a civilization on the edge of oblivion.

FACE UNIMAGINABLE HORRORS

Engage in mind-bending encounters that blur the lines between reality and illusion. Expect the unexpected as you delve deeper into the dark mysteries of Nova Locus and The Nexus.

Make choices that will impact the fate of Nova Locus and its inhabitants. Every decision you make will shape the outcome of the story, leading to multiple potential endings.

Discover artifacts and relics from an eldritch realm. These mysterious items hold the key to understanding the cosmic horros that threaten the world of Eventide.
